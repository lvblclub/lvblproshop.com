03 — Our Story

More than
a match.

LVBL started with a simple idea: tennis should be more social, more accessible, and a lot more fun. What began as a fast-paced, community-driven Liveball format has grown into a nationwide network of players, coaches, and events — bringing energy back to courts across cities like Los Angeles, New York, Miami, and beyond.

The community

At its core, LVBL is about connection — creating environments where players of all levels can show up, compete, improve, and be part of something bigger than just a match.

We host clinics, tournaments, leagues, and socials across all three racquet sports. From first-timers to 4.5s, there's a place on the court for everyone.

The pro shop

The LVBL Pro Shop is a natural extension of that vision. As our community has grown, so has the demand for a retail experience that reflects the same ethos: curated, elevated, and built for modern players.

Nowhere is that momentum more visible than in the Pacific Northwest, where we've rapidly expanded our footprint with a growing number of pro shop locations — each designed to feel like a neighborhood clubhouse while maintaining a consistent LVBL identity.

We're rethinking the traditional tennis shop — blending premium gear with a community-first approach, and bringing it directly into the environments where our players already connect. Whether online or inside one of our partner locations, the LVBL Pro Shop is more than a place to buy equipment — it's a physical expression of the community we've built, and a platform for where the game is going next.
